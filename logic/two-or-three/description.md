Write a function `two_or_three(n: int) -> bool` that takes an integer `n` and returns `True` if `n` is a multiple of 2 or a multiple of 3, **but not both**.

For example:
- `two_or_three(6) → False` as 6 is a multiple of both 2 and 3.
- `two_or_three(4) → True` as 4 is a multiple of 2 and not 3.
- `two_or_three(9) → False` as 9 is a multiple of 3 and not 2.
